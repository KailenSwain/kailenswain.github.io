Freelance Product Design Project

I was approached for a freelance project through a mutual connection. I had never done freelance design and saw this as an opportunity to try something new. My client was a pair of partners who were looking for a product designer to help them flush out their concepts for a sort of e-commerce platform combined with social media.

Role

Freelance Designer

Timeframe

August 2022-October 2022

Platform

iOS Native

Project Summary

A peer-to-peer E-commerce platform based on genuine testimonials and user experiences. I was an acting design partner and product designer, helping my clients with brainstorming features, wire-framing, and creating MVP design and mockups to pitch with.

Getting Started

The design work started completely from scratch.

I was brought up to speed on the general concept, and given opportunities to provide my perspective and feedback. I started with creating some storyboards as well as a first stab at mapping out the system design of a mobile app (desired platform by clients)

Research

“In hindsight, my least favorite part about this project was the lack of research conducted.”

I should have pushed my clients harder for more time for research, but they cited time constraints as a reason against it. The only research I really conducted was looking into similar products for their design patterns and aesthetics.

Some of products in similar spaces to my client's concept that I researched for patterns.

Process

I started with medium fidelity wireframes based on how I envisioned the product. These wireframes were a key point the clients to start considering the look and feel of the prototype.

The clients pushed for something sleek and polished early on, but I pushed back as I considered it more important to dial in the user experience before we start to focus on aesthetics and fidelity of the mockup.

The screens below show some of the exploration for a points/reward system, as well as some quick color treatment.

As the designs started to come together, my clients and I were able to determine the key micro-interactions of the product, and what I needed to design solutions for. My main deliverable was a figma prototype that was something between a wireframe and high fidelity mockup.

Taking on a freelance product project was an amazing learning experience for me, but I came to the conclusion that freelance + full-time design job is not a long-term sustainable workload for me.
